Our Story

How did you meet?

We met on Clifton 4th beach one Friday afternoon during one of the regular frisbee socials. It was Melissa’s first time attending a Frisbee event. Daylan spotted her walking with a friend from across the beach, and a flame was ignited. In an attempt to get her attention, he threw a frisbee right at her. Although she was oblivious to his valiant attempt, Daylan remained persistent. After the Frisbee the group moved to a nearby restaurant to get some dinner. Daylan strategically planted himself at the end of the table next to where Melissa was sitting. It wasn’t long until they found themselves engrossed in conversation with each other the whole evening . There may as well have been no one else at the table. Numbers were exchanged, and the rest was history.


Where did you grow up?

Daylan was born at Constantiaberg Hospital and grew up in Cape Town his entire life. He has lived in several places, from Simon’s Town, to Noordhoek, and even Southampton in the UK. He now happily resides on Sorrento Farm in Dassenberg. Melissa was born in Pretoria, and grew up in Centurion. She did her community service in Nelspruit, Mpumalanga, after which getting an amazing opportunity to live and work in Cape Town in 2020. Now she will move to her forever home on Sorrento Farm with Daylan. The city girl will soon become a real plaas meisie.


Describe your most special memory or adventure together.

Daylan: Wow, it's not possible to choose one memory. It's been one long and growing adventure full of special memories together. Our first real adventure was renting bikes and riding the promenade together where I first noticed how giddy and goofy we could be with each other. After that we got some ice-cream in seapoint which is still our favourite little place to get a sweet treat.

Melissa: We went to a dear friend’s wedding in Keimoes, Northern Cape. We were trusting google maps to get us there safely, but about five hours in to the road trip, we ended up on a dirt road for almost two hours! It was an unrelenting, treacherous, never-ending, abandoned stretch of nothingness. Buuuut… it was also so stunningly beautiful and the landscapes around us were breathtaking. Once we overcame our fears and irritations, and embraced the moment, it was truly a wonderful adventure with Daylan. Even though the road was scary, bumpy and hot, the conversations that were had in the car and the beautiful views we saw, will definitely stay in my heart forever.


Describe your partners best traits.

Daylan: One of Melissa's best traits is her ability to find the positive in any negative situation and uplift an atmosphere. Melissa has a personality that brings out the best in whoever she encounters, she always wants to see the best for her loved one’s, and has the most positive uplifting type of love I have ever experienced. She is always loyal to her word and has an above and beyond way of serving others. I love watching her inquisitive mind working at solving a problem whether someone else's problem or hers. She has a go getter way of approach to new seasons and loves bringing positive change into any environment.

Melissa: Daylan is extremely patient, gentle and kind. He is a man of integrity and cannot tell a lie, not even a small one. He is a very present and emotionally mature partner. He always wants to know how he can support me or what he can do to meet my needs or make life better for me, even in the smallest of ways. He is reliable and always does what he says he will do, and he is very determined once he has decided on something. He listens well and genuinely cares for those around him. He also has a very cute and goofy personality that makes me smile everyday. He is amazingly talented in so many ways, I am constantly learning about new things he can do or surprised by how innovative and creative his mind can be.


Describe your partners more ummm.. quirky traits ;)

Daylan: Melisse has what I like to call "a closure" problem. From peanut butter lid, draws, tomato sauce lid, fridge door to even toothpaste cap she just can’t close it.  (Please I need to understand this.) She also will drive past 5 parking spots before parking in a parking that’s least convenient, almost like when a dog spins around 3 times before actually laying down. She has this specific smile and little clap she does only when she is very very happy. This is actually extremely cute lol.

Melissa: Daylan is extremely pedantic about the way that things are arranged in his house and where things belong. I’ve had to learn to put something back EXACTLY the way I found it after using it, to the exact millimetre (lol). Daylan eats extremely slowly and I’m always left at the dining table staring at him because I’ve finished all my food by the time he starts eating (I like my food hot and he likes his food three days old..hehehe). However, if it's a packet of sweets or a chocolate bar, he eats the whole thing in one bite, so I have to act quicky if I plan on sharing any of that with him! Iv’e also realised that Daylan is prone to injury, accidents and the man flu. Iv’e had to nurse him several times during the course of our relationship, and I must say that he can be quite a difficult patient... ;P


What hobbies do you enjoy together?

We enjoy being active and have developed a nice routine of going to the gym together a few days a week. On the weekends however, you will find us trying out different ice cream parlours and grabbing a New York Slice, yum! We enjoy going on bike rides together and we often take a cruise to find a new scenic lunch spot. We love the beach but we hate the sand lol. We often go for walks on the beach, but we would definitely rather swim in a pool. Other than this, we are just enjoying all the little things that life has to offer together.


How did you know she is the one?

Daylan: I first started thinking she was the one when I noticed I could be fully myself no matter how goofy or silly I was, she just loved me more for it. I also found no fault with who she was because I could love her 100% so naturally for who she was. I knew 100% that she was the one when I could see how we handled each and every situation we crossed and only wanted the best for each other and to uplift each other and grow together.


How did you know he is the one?

Melissa: There is not one specific thing or moment where I knew Daylan was the one. What I do know is that I prayed long and hard for a partner like Daylan. When he entered my life, I felt a deep sense of knowing within me that he would be playing a significant part of my life and that he was here to stay. I felt the peace of the Lord with him from our very first date, up until now.


What do you look forwards to the most in marriage?

Daylan:  I look forwards to having my best friend come home to me every day and to make food together and do life together. It’s about the simple things for me. I look forward to going on adventures with my best friend. I look forwards to experiencing new chapters of life like having kids and building a big, beautiful home together

Melissa: I look forward to building our home up and decorating it with Daylan. I look forward to having a chilled and stable home life. It is from this place of love and stability where a person can truly flourish and become the best version of themselves. I definitely look forward to planning our overseas trip very soon, we’ve been talking about this since we started dating. The thing I look forward to the most is having my partner in crime there by my side all the time. When I have a good day, he will be there to share the joy with, and when I have a bad day, he will still there to confide in.
